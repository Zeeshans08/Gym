Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Omkar Fitness Gym has grown from a small local gym to a premier fitness destination with state-of-the-art facilities and world-class trainers.

Our mission is to provide an inclusive environment where people of all fitness levels can achieve their health and wellness goals.

Our Facilities

Spread over 15,000 square feet, our facility offers the latest cardio and strength training equipment, dedicated functional training zones, and specialized areas for boxing and martial arts.

We also feature a recovery lounge with massage chairs and a smoothie bar to refuel after your workout.
